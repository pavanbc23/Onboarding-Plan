Accenture | Employee Onboarding Program

DaysEvent NameDescriptionDate
Day 1Welcome to AccentureInduction event. The new employees will be familiarized with Accenture's way of working, its vision, and what to expect with respect to career goals. Interesting sessions will be planned based on the availability of speakers. @Oct 12, 2020
Day 2Buddy AssignmentPractice Induction CallThe communication team will send out an email, assigning a buddy to the employee. The buddy will get in touch with the employee and assist him/her with their daily tasks, answering any questions, helping them network, etc. A call will also be set up with the upper management team. The idea is to help the new employee get more clarity on the practice within Accenture that they now belong to.@Oct 13, 2020
Day 3Coach MeetupQ & A SessionThe new employee will meet with the coach, another experienced practitioner, who will provide more information on what to expect in the coming days. This information may include the practice's team structure, project structure, etc. The employees (3 to 5 on one meetup) will get to ask any questions and get them clarified.@Oct 14, 2020
Day 4Accenture Groups IntroductionResource Manager ConnectWellbeing ConnectWellbeing connects include planned and structured connects between new employees and team members with a focus on physical and mental wellbeing, work-life balance, best practices, ideas, suggestions, and musings. The new employees will also meet with the champions of different Accenture groups based on their preferences such as reading, playing music, learning new technologies, etc. This allows them to find a safe space within the organization to hone their extracurricular skills. The new employees will also meet with the resource manager to discuss the next steps with respect to project allocation, provided that they are not assigned to a project yet.@Oct 15, 2020
Day 5Coach MeetupProject IntroductionTeam Engagement SessionThe team engagement session is an interactive session among practitioners from all the locations. The session will be focused on employees discussing exciting new topics, creative trends in the market, etc. A new-employee introduction segment will be a part of the session. This will provide a stage for the new employees to network with teams across the country. The second meetup with the coach will be focused on setting up a learning goal for the new employee. Based on this discussion, a learning path will be designed, and the employee can start taking the required courses to advance in their career levels. The project introduction call will allow the employee to get in touch with the project manager to understand the project scope, objectives, and functionalities.@Oct 16, 2020
Day 6Project Buddy AssignmentProject Manager MeetupProject Team InductionThe project manager meetup will allow the employee to get in touch with the onsite team. Any questions on the project will be answered on this call. This will be followed up with a project team induction meeting. An experienced practitioner from the project will be assigned as a project buddy. He/she will be responsible for providing project related documents, project onboarding decks, client materials, etc. The new employee will also be made aware of the day to day activities and the team cadences.@Oct 19, 2020
Day 7Upper Management ConnectUpper management meetings will be scheduled for the new employees to connect with their superiours. These happen outside the project between delivery managers and new practitioners. The idea is to provide more information on the company, how the practice is doing, what are the future plans etc. This will build a good relationship between the practice heads and the employees.@Oct 20, 2020
Day 8No EventsAdd activities as needed@Oct 21, 2020
Day 9No EventsAdd activities as needed@Oct 22, 2020
Day 10Friday Fun ActivityA fun Friday event will be scheduled across teams, marking the end of week two. The communication team will send out a mailer, covering the contact details of all the teams, such as IT, HR, Finance, etc. This is to ensure that the new practitioners have all the required information to start helping themselves and get comfortable.@Oct 23, 2020
